Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle

Author: Arthur Conan Doyle

Genre: Mystery, Detective Fiction

Summary: The adventures of Sherlock Holmes, a brilliant detective, and his friend Dr. Watson as they solve various mysteries.

Review: The Sherlock Holmes series is a captivating collection of detective stories. Doyle's intricate plots and Holmes' keen observational skills make each story a delight to read.

Rating: 5/5

Published: 1887-1927

The Notebook by Nicholas Sparks

Author: Nicholas Sparks

Genre: Romance, Drama

Summary: A moving tale of enduring love between Noah and Allie, who are separated by circumstances but find their way back to each other.

Review: The Notebook is a heartwarming and emotional love story. Sparks' storytelling and the depth of the characters' love make it a timeless romance.

Rating: 4.5/5

Published: 1996

Me Before You by Jojo Moyes

Author: Jojo Moyes

Genre: Romance, Drama

Summary: The story of Louisa Clark, who becomes a caregiver for Will Traynor, a wealthy young banker left paralyzed after an accident, and how their lives change forever.

Review: Me Before You is a touching and thought-provoking novel. Moyes explores complex themes of love, loss, and the value of life with sensitivity and grace.

Rating: 4/5

Published: 2012

The Silence of the Lambs by Thomas Harris

Author: Thomas Harris

Genre: Thriller, Crime

Summary: FBI trainee Clarice Starling seeks the help of imprisoned cannibalistic serial killer Dr. Hannibal Lecter to catch another serial killer, Buffalo Bill.

Review: The Silence of the Lambs is a chilling and masterfully crafted thriller. Harris' characters, especially Lecter, are unforgettable, and the plot keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

Rating: 5/5

Published: 1988
